# Inkwise

> Design your tattoo. Before the needle.

AI tattoo designer with flash from actual shops. See it on your skin before you commit.

## What you get

- **Describe the idea** — A koi fish. A line-work wolf. A memorial for your grandma. We sketch it.
- **Try it on your skin** — AR preview on your arm, shoulder, back. See it at scale before the chair.
- **Book a nearby shop** — Connect with artists in your city who can execute the design.

## Category

Consumer AI. Part of a 50-product exploration of high-demand consumer and SMB markets.
